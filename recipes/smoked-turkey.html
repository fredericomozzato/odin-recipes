Smoked Turkey Sandwich

A classic sandwich made with sourdough bread, smoked turkey, cheese and salad. As always, the secret to a good sandwich, is good ingredients. Try to get a good smoked turkey and the better cheese you can afford. For the salad we reccomend american lettuce for the crunchiness.

    Ingredients
  • 2 slices of sourdough bread, around 2cm thick
  • Sliced smoked turkey
  • Good quality cheese
  • Red onion
  • Pickles
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Butter

    Instructions
  1. Spread the butter in the slices of bread and toast it in a pan in low heat until golden. For extra an crunch you can toast both sides
  2. In the same pan lightly toast the slices of turkey and put the cheese on top to melt
  3. Spread a little mustard in one of the slices of bread and mayo in the other
  4. Place the sliced onion, the pickles and tomato on top of the mustard slice
  5. When the cheese melts place the turkey and on top of the salad
  6. Place the lettuce on top of the turkey and cheese and close the sandwich with the mayo slice of bread

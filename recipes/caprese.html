Cream Cheese Caprese

The classic caprese sandwich with a creamy touch! We'll use the ciabatta bread but you can use any bread you like for this one (whole grain bread makes a nice choice too!). The secret is to use good quality cream cheese and to give an extra crunch we also put toasted almonds and brazillian nuts, but this is totally optional.

    Ingredients
  • Ciabatta
  • Cream cheese
  • Coleworth
  • Grape tomato
  • Mozzarella
  • Olive oil
  • Optional - Grated parmesan cheese
  • Optional - Almonds
  • Optional - Brazillian nut

    Instructions
  1. Slice the ciabatta in half and pour a little olive oil on each side. Lightly toat it ina pan
  2. Spread the cream cheese on both halves of bread
  3. Add the coleworth, slice the grape tomatoes in half and place them on top of the coleworth
  4. Slice the Mozzarella and place it on top of the salad. If you opt for the parmesan cheese add a little on top of the mozzarella
  5. If you'd like to use the nuts you can place them on top of the mozzarella
  6. Close the sandwich and enjoy

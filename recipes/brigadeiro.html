Brigadeiro

Brigadeiro is one of the most popular brazillian candies. It's commonly seen at birthday parties an it consists of condensend milk and butter mixed with powdered chocolate or cocoa, then rolled in little balls and covered with sprinkled chocolate.

    Ingredients
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • 40g of powdered chocolate. If you're using powdered cocoa use 20 to 30g
  • 2 cups of sprinkled chocolate

  1. Add the condensed milk, the butter and the powdered chocolate to a deep pan
  2. Cook in medium heat until the mixture stop sticking to the bottom of the pan
  3. Take out of the pan and put it in a bowl to let it cool down
  4. Put the sprinkled chocolate in a plate
  5. Model the mixture in little balls with your hands and then roll it in the sprinkle chocolate until it's totally covered
